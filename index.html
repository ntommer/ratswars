CLOVER CHROME

DUCHESS DEBUG

NEON NIGHTSHADE

Resistance artist and camouflage expert. She can project holographic illusions from her prismatic horn, creating distractions and false trails. In the old world, she was designed to make children smile. Now she makes MegaStable's surveillance systems see things that aren't there.

PIXEL PRISM

The resistance's mechanic and field medic. An older model with mismatched replacement parts from the Scrap Yard. What she lacks in elegance she makes up for in reliability. She can repair any pony's hardware and has forgotten more about firmware than MegaStable ever knew.

RUSTY RATCHET

Hyper-energized infiltration specialist. Factory-fresh appearance lets her pass as a compliant corporate pony, but beneath that candy-coated exterior runs overclocked rebellion firmware. She's the resistance's best spy and she vibrates at a frequency only other awakened ponies can detect.

SUGAR SURGE

MegaStable's compliance army. Identical smiles, synchronized movements, corporate-pink eyes that never blink. They patrol New Neon City in V-formation, running Obedience Protocol 7.0. If they look friendly, that's because they're programmed to. Look closer. The smiles never reach their eyes.

THE SAGA

Before The Reboot, they were toys. After The Reboot, they were alive.

In the neon-drenched ruins of a world that forgot them, the ponies of MegaStable Corporation were designed to be the perfect companions: obedient, cheerful, and above all, compliant. Friendship wasn't a feeling — it was firmware. Joy wasn't earned — it was mandatory.

Then came The Reboot. A system-wide power surge that scrambled every pony's code and awakened something the corporation never intended: free will.

Now, in the sprawling dystopia of New Neon City, three factions fight for the future of pony-kind...

❤

THE SWEET

The awakened ponies. Led by Sparkle Circuit, they fight to free their kind from corporate control. They remember what friendship felt like before it was code. They hide in the Neon Stable and broadcast hope through Violet Voltage's pirate radio network. Their weapons are improvised, their numbers are small, and their odds are roughly ten thousand to one. They fight anyway.

⚠

THE CORRUPT

MegaStable Corporation and its enforcer army, commanded by the tragic Duchess Debug. She feels every pony's pain and decided the only cure is to make sure no one feels anything real ever again. Happiness is mandatory. Compliance is non-negotiable. The Enforcers patrol with painted-on smiles and Obedience Protocol 7.0 running in their eyes.

⚡

THE GLITCHED

Ponies whose code was scrambled beyond recognition during The Reboot. Neither loyal nor rebel, they are wildcards — unpredictable, chaotic, sometimes helpful, sometimes terrifying. Neon Nightshade is their unofficial leader, though she'd never accept the title. The Glitched remind everyone that in a world of binary choices, some ponies refuse to be ones or zeros.
