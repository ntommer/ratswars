HOW TO PLAY

  1. Each player holds 6 character cards
  2. Pick a stat — JU-JU, COMBAT, or CUNNING
  3. Higher stat wins both cards
  4. Ties: each player keeps their card
  5. First to collect all 12 cards wins the galaxy!
